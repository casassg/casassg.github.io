---
title: "To new beginnings"
description: "Moving to a new blog."
---

I've decided to start writing again. To be honest, I've never been a huge fan of writing in general; when I was young, it was the task I dreaded most. However, I've come to a place where I realize there is value in sharing —or at least capturing— some of the thoughts that have been bouncing around in my head.

Especially now, in the age of AI, human writing feels like both an underestimated effort and a uniquely valuable pursuit that requires real dedication.

Admittedly, my writing is still a work in progress, so I may use AI (mostly Gemini, since it tends to adapt better to my thought process) to help me tweak and iterate on my drafts. My goal has never been to become a professional writer (code is the closest thing I write well) but rather to share ideas.

Finally, to make things easier on myself, I've made it simpler to share quotes and other quick thoughts I find useful. By embracing shorter formats, I hope to lower the activation energy required to go from a raw idea to a published post.
